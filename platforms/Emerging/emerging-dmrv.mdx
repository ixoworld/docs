This page documents the **Emerging Household Energy** scope. Use it to run household-energy monitoring and reporting workflows on top of **Emerging Platform dMRV** capabilities.

<Info>
  Platform reference: `/platforms/Emerging/dmrv`
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before you implement solution dMRV, confirm:

- identity and entity setup is complete
- household and device registration flows are available
- credential issuance flow is configured

## Implementation flow

<Steps>
<Step title="Set up households and devices">
Register household and device entities, then bind them to monitoring scope.

- Household setup: `/platforms/Emerging/household-monitoring`
- Device telemetry setup: `/platforms/Emerging/stove-use-monitoring`
</Step>

<Step title="Collect and validate monitoring data">
Ingest monitored data for the reporting period and run verification checks for completeness and consistency.

- Supplementary validation inputs: `/platforms/Emerging/kpt`
- Survey support data: `/platforms/Emerging/qualitative-surveys`
</Step>

<Step title="Generate household reporting outputs">
Aggregate accepted evidence into household reporting outputs for downstream use.

- Reporting workflow: `/platforms/Emerging/household-reporting`
</Step>

<Step title="Issue credentials and track outcomes">
Issue or update credentials tied to verified outcomes and preserve traceability in registry-linked state.

- Credential lifecycle: `/platforms/Emerging/credential-issuance`
- Certification-specific references: `/platforms/Emerging/itmo-credentials`
</Step>
</Steps>
