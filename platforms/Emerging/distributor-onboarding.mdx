This page documents **Emerging Household Energy** distributor onboarding. It replaces internal test-flow detail with a public checklist for implementation teams.

## Before you start

- Confirm access to required integration environments.
- Confirm project, organization, and agent identities are ready.
- Confirm registry and credential issuance flows are available.

## Onboarding checklist

1. Register distributor organization identity.
2. Configure distributor permissions and operational roles.
3. Link distributor to project and reporting workflows.
4. Validate claim submission and credential verification paths.
5. Run a dry-run onboarding and verify expected outputs.

## Verification criteria

- distributor can submit allowed records
- distributor records resolve to expected project scope
- downstream reporting includes distributor-linked events

## Outcome

After this guide, a distributor is ready to:

- register as an authorized participant
- submit required onboarding evidence
- participate in household-energy distribution and reporting workflows

## Prerequisites

- Access to solution operator workflow and required permissions
- Identity setup completed for the onboarding organization
- Required supporting documentation available

## Steps

1. Register distributor identity and controller details.
2. Submit onboarding profile and required documentation.
3. Complete verification checks and approval workflow.
4. Link distributor to the relevant project or delivery scope.
5. Confirm access to reporting and claim submission functions.
