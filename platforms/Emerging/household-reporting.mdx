This page documents **Emerging Household Energy** reporting workflows. It explains how to aggregate household data from monitoring streams and produce reporting outputs for verification and program operations.

## Reporting scope

Household reporting combines:

- household and device identifiers
- stove-use and survey evidence
- claim verification status
- reporting-period summaries

## Recommended reporting flow

1. Confirm household and device registration is complete.
2. Collect monitoring evidence for the reporting period.
3. Run verification checks and resolve anomalies.
4. Generate report outputs and link supporting claims.

## Inputs to include

- household DID and related device DIDs
- reporting period start and end
- evidence references and verification status
- summary metrics used by your implementation

This page documents the **Emerging Household Energy** scope. Use it to produce household-level reporting outputs from monitored household and device activity.

## What household reporting does

Household reporting aggregates verified activity into reporting periods so you can:

- summarize household usage and participation state
- trace evidence back to monitored records
- support dMRV and downstream certification workflows

## Prerequisites

- Household and device registration completed
- Monitoring data available for the target period
- Claim or credential workflows configured

## Reporting workflow

1. Select reporting period and household scope.
2. Aggregate monitored records and linked evidence.
3. Validate data completeness and consistency.
4. Generate a report artifact and verification metadata.
5. Submit to the next step in your dMRV or certification process.
