## Selection guide

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use IXO MultiClient SDK" icon="layer-group">
    Choose this when you need one SDK surface across multiple IXO services.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use IXO Matrix Client SDK" icon="message">
    Choose this when your app centers on encrypted rooms, messaging, and stateful data exchange.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Agentic Oracles SDK" icon="sparkles">
    Choose this when your workflow relies on agent-driven verification and automation.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
