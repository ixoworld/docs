## Overview

IXO Oracles represent a new paradigm in blockchain oracle services by combining Web3 capabilities with agentic AI. These autonomous, data-driven entities go beyond traditional oracles by providing:

- Self-Sovereign Identity through DIDs
- NFT-Based Domain Ownership
- AI-Driven Decision Making
- Verifiable Claims and Impacts

<Note>
IXO Oracles bridge the gap between on-chain and off-chain realities while ensuring reliability, transparency, and responsible AI practices.
</Note>
