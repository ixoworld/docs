<Note>
  The IXO Spatial Web is a decentralized platform that enables the creation and management of cognitive digital twins for real-world systems, powered by blockchain networks, secure data storage, and AI oracles.
</Note>

## Digital Twins

Digital twins are verifiable digital representations of real-world entities and systems. Each digital twin has:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Decentralized Identity" icon="fingerprint">
    - W3C DID (Decentralized Identifier) for unique identification
    - Cryptographic verification capabilities
    - Self-sovereign control through key management
    - Verifiable credentials for claims and attributes
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Domain Properties" icon="cube">
    - Entity type classification
    - Controller assignments
    - Service endpoints
    - Relationship mappings
    - Resource linkages
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
