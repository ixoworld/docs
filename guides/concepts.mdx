## Overview

<Note>
The Emerging Platform enables decentralized measurement, reporting, and verification (dMRV) of climate impacts using blockchain, IoT, and AI technologies.
</Note>

## Platform Architecture

<CardGroup>

<Card title="Blockchain Layer" icon="cube">
  Immutable ledger for climate impact data and transactions
</Card>

<Card title="Data Layer" icon="database">
  IoT integration and secure data storage
</Card>

<Card title="Integration Layer" icon="plug">
  APIs and interfaces for external systems
</Card>

<Card title="AI Layer" icon="robot">
  Oracle services and automated verification
</Card>

</CardGroup>

## Key Features

### Digital MRV (dMRV)

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Data Collection">
- IoT device integration
- Real-time monitoring
- Standardized reporting
- Secure validation
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verification">
- Automated checks
- Oracle services
- Cross-validation
- Audit trails
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

### Digital Certification

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Credentials">
- W3C Verifiable Credentials
- Digital ITMO certificates
- Proof of outcome claims
- Selective disclosure
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Verification">
- Data provenance (Verifiable Claims)
- Cryptographic proofs
- Web of Trust (Verifiable Credentials)
- Revocation checking
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

### Digital Registry

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Asset Management">
- Climate impact tracking
- Ownership records
- Transfer history
- Compliance with international standards
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Integration">
- Carbon markets
- National registries
- Trading platforms
- Digital Finance Applications
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Data Model

<ResponseField name="Entities" type="object">
  Digital twins of real-world objects (households, devices, organizations)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Claims" type="object">
  Verifiable statements about entities (measurements, events)
</ResponseField>

<ResponseField name="Credentials" type="object">
  Certified collections of claims (certificates, authorizations)
</ResponseField>
